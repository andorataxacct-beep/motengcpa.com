About Moteng CPA

Empowering Business Owners With Financial Clarity and Confidence.

Moteng CPA helps business owners and organizational leaders understand their numbers, improve cash flow, plan taxes, and make decisions with more confidence.

Our Focus

Financial support for the businesses and organizations that keep communities moving.

Lasting financial success starts with clarity. Moteng CPA provides accounting, bookkeeping, tax preparation, tax planning, audit readiness, assurance, and advisory support for owners and leaders who need clean records and practical guidance.

Our work is built around the realities of construction, nonprofit operations, healthcare, transportation, government contracting, and growing businesses where cash flow, compliance, reporting, and timing all matter.

Who We Serve

Practical financial guidance for hardworking owners and leaders.

Our clients are builders managing job sites, nonprofit teams working around funding deadlines, healthcare professionals balancing care with compliance, transportation businesses navigating rising costs, and owners who need clearer financial direction.

We meet clients where they are with customized support that simplifies the numbers, strengthens decision-making, and helps them focus on growth, impact, and building what matters most.
