Government Contracting & Teaming

Government agencies need financial professionals who understand two worlds at once: the technical discipline of accounting and audit work, and the procurement environment in which that work is delivered.

Moteng CPA, PLLC is a Texas-licensed CPA firm registered in SAM.gov and actively serving the government marketplace. We provide accounting, financial management, internal audit support, and compliance services to federal, state, and local agencies, both as a prime contractor and as a teaming partner to other firms.

Core Competencies

Audit Readiness and Financial Compliance

Agencies and the organizations they fund face constant audit exposure: financial statement audits, single audits, inspector general reviews, and program-specific compliance examinations. We help organizations prepare, respond, and improve.

This work draws on our broader audit readiness practice and our attest and assurance experience as a licensed CPA firm. We understand what examiners look for because examination standards are our profession's home ground.

Why Agencies Work With Small Firms

A small firm on a government engagement means the professionals named in the proposal are the professionals doing the work. No handoff to junior staff after award, no rotating cast of team members to re-brief.

Working with Moteng CPA, agencies get:

  • Direct access to the firm's principal on every engagement
  • Licensed CPA and Enrolled Agent credentials on the work itself, not just the letterhead
  • Responsiveness that large-firm structures cannot match
  • Engagement teams assembled specifically for each contract's requirements
  • Clear, well-documented deliverables built for the government environment

Teaming and Subcontracting

Government work is a team sport, and we approach it that way. Moteng CPA pursues opportunities as a prime contractor and partners with other firms when a combined team serves the requirement better.

We welcome teaming conversations with:

  • Prime contractors seeking a licensed CPA firm as a subcontractor for accounting, audit support, or financial compliance workstreams
  • Small businesses looking for a financial services partner to strengthen a pursuit
  • Firms that need CPA or Enrolled Agent credentials on their team to meet solicitation requirements
  • Consulting firms whose engagements have grown to include financial workstreams

We structure teaming relationships to fit the opportunity: subcontracts, teaming agreements, or joint pursuits. If you have a solicitation in hand and need a financial partner, reach out with the requirement and we will respond quickly.

  • State agencies, including Texas state government
  • Local governments and special districts
  • Prime contractors building teams for financial and audit-related requirements
  • Nonprofit organizations managing federal, state, or local government funding
